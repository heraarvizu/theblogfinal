My Story

As a kid I'd expect you to have you're own fascination with anything in this world. Well mine was the creation of character out of clay or any material in relation of it. How can someone put so much detail into a figure while making it move like a real person? It didn't only catch my interest by how a movie or show was created but how satisfying it must be to be hands on and manipulating the material of the character the way you imagine it. I appreciated the process of time it took to build a character and have it can feel so personal to your own personality. To then see the outcome become so meaningful behind how a storyline can be applied to our own lives through many positive messages. This is for the young kids who image a world of thier own creation, and to young me (Herita) for her commitment and ambition to live out every dream she had
